Our Story

If I could order the perfect guy from a catelog, he still wouldn't compare to Shane. I'm so happy to be on this adventure called life together.

While celebrating our engagement together one of my close friends stated,

"When we graduated college I would have told you that Lauren would be the last of us to get married. When I met Shane, I knew she'd be the first."

Shane has the biggest heart in the world. He not only supports me in everything I do, but constantly pushes me to be better. I could not have gotten luckier.

I am so excited to spend the rest of my life as an adventure with this man.

Lauren pushes me every single day to be the best version of myself I can be

I could not have asked for a better partner in her. She is someone who knows my true desires, fears, thoughts, and intracacies. Without her, I don't feel I could truly be myself.

I will rest easy knowing I found the other half of me, I didn't even know I was missing.
